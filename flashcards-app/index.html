Biology 101

15 cards • Last studied: 2 days ago

What is photosynthesis?

Process by which plants convert light into chemical energy.

What is mitochondria?

The powerhouse of the cell; produces ATP for energy.

What is DNA?

Deoxyribonucleic acid; carries genetic instructions for life.

What is osmosis?

Movement of water across a semipermeable membrane.

Define metabolism.

Chemical processes that maintain life and convert nutrients to energy.

What is homeostasis?

Maintenance of stable internal conditions in living organisms.
